Style choices

The Asciidoc documentation gives guidance on how to write different types of semantic content. This section attempts to offer guidance on when to use specific elements.

Admonitions

The Asciidoc documentation does explain the difference between Caution and Warning. We expand on that, inspired by other documentation guidelines.

Neutral information

Use a NOTE to inform the reader of something even when skimming a page.

Positive consequences

Negative consequences
Use a TIP to help a reader benefit from potential added value, or to explore a new direction.
Use a CAUTION note to advise the reader to act carefully (i.e., exercise care), to avoid errors or mistakes.
Use an IMPORTANT note to share crucial information for the reader to be succesful.
Use a WARNING to inform the reader of danger, harm, or severe consequences that exist.
